About Us

Welcome to Mocking Bird

We envision a world in which all children can pursue a quality education that enables them to reach their full potential and contribute to their communities and the world. Mocking Bird seeks to transform the lives of millions of children in low-income communities by focusing on literacy and gender equality in education. Working in collaboration with local communities, partner organizations and governments, we develop literacy skills and a habit of reading among primary school children, and support girls to complete secondary school with the relevant life skills to succeed in school and beyond.
